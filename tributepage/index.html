Dr. Norman Borlaug

The man who saved a billion lives

Here's a timeline of Dr. Borlaug's life:

  • 1914: Born in Cresco, Iowa.
  • 1944: Joined the Rockefeller Foundation in Mexico to work on wheat improvement.
  • 1970: Won the Nobel Peace Prize for his efforts in combating world hunger.
  • 2009: Passed away, leaving behind a legacy of fighting global hunger.
